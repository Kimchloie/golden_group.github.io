Content

Our group decided to choose Vlog as a youtube content to use for the barangay Sitio Bebe,
the need of our audience is an awareness and update of the Sitio Bebe. This content will
present the ongoing happenings of the Sitio Bebe, that will make the viewers/audience
beware of the situation there. It can show the content about the people, the place, and
the problem of the community. This content can also be used for promotion or
to broadcast our possible solution to make the problem solved and to have more
people to help the community.
